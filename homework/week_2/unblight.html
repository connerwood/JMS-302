Unblight Macon

Macon, GA hosted an unconference (an organic conference where attendees do most of the talking) August 14th and 15thon housing blight in Macon. It brought members of the community together for a central purpose-to talk about ways to measure blight in hopes of one day reducing it.
According to an article written by the Telegraph, Mayor Robert Reichert said, It's hard to raise children up right in a dilapidated neighborhood...it breaks the spirit.


Reversing blight, together.

Blight has many definitions. The Center for Collaborative Journalism, along with other community and national sponsors, is kickstarting a mission to end blight. The initiave will be called UB.
